<HEADLINE>
US pleased with new talks with Cuba: Clinton
</HEADLINE>
<DATELINE>
San Salvador, May 31, 2009 (AFP)
</DATELINE>
<TEXT>
<P>
US Secretary of State Hillary Clinton said here Sunday the United States is
pleased that Cuba has agreed to resume talks on migration issues and to start
discussions on direct mail service.
</P>
<P>
"We're very pleased that the Cuban government has agreed to talks on migration
and direct mail links," Clinton told reporters after US officials announced the
new discussions that continue a US-Cuban thaw in ties.
</P>
